Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Hell Jump

Last Updated: May 13th, 2026

1. Data Collection

Hell Jump does not collect, and never will collect, any personal information directly from our users during gameplay. The app itself is designed to be played without an account.

2. Third-Party Services (Ads)

We use Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your device settings

You can view Google's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Note: Ads are served only on mobile platforms such as Android and iOS

3. Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your region), please ask a parent or guardian before playing. Note: The age guidance below applies to mobile (Android/iOS) users only, as ads are only shown on mobile platforms.

4. Your Choices

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by:

  • In-Game: Open Settings → Ads → Toggle "Disable Ads" your game will close
  • Device Settings: Manage ad preferences in your device's privacy settings
  • Google: Visit Ad Center to manage your Google ad preferences

5. Contact & Support

If you choose to use our Help Center, you may sign in using third-party services (Google or GitHub). In this case:

  • We only access the basic profile information provided by the service (such as your email and name) to to manage posted issues.
  • We do not share this data with anyone else.
  • The Help Center is completely optional and not required to play the game.
  • We do not store your GitHub/Google account password. Authentication is handled entirely by the third-party service.

You can also contact us directly via email.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Hell Jump means you accept the updated policy.

7. Data Retention

AdMob data is retained per Google's policies. We retain no user data ourselves.

8. GDPR Compliance

For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We ensure that:

  • Consent: We request your explicit consent to show personalized ads through a Consent Management Platform (CMP).
  • Transparency: You can review, modify, or revoke your consent at any time via the settings menu in the game.
  • Data Control: We only process data necessary for the functionality of the game and our advertising partners.
