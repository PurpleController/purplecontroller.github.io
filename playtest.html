Program Info

Terms of the Playtester Program

1. Deadline
Applications are open until 25 June 2026. After this date, submissions will not be accepted.

2. Voluntary Participation
This is a voluntary playtesting program. You are not an employee or contractor of AramCZGames.

3. No Payment
Playtesters will not receive any payment or financial compensation.

4. Credits
Playtesters may be credited in the game under “Special Thanks” or “Playtesters”.

To be guaranteed credit, you must actively participate and complete at least the Deep Hell chapter during your testing period.

5. Confidentiality
You must not share, stream, record, or distribute any test builds or unreleased content.

6. Feedback Usage
Any feedback you submit may be used to improve Hell Jump without compensation.

7. Removal
You may be removed from the program at any time for violating these terms or for testing needs.

8. No Guarantee
Participation does not guarantee access to all builds, future tests, or inclusion in credits.

9. Agreement
By continuing, you confirm that you understand and accept these terms.
